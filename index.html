Dave Chappelle

The man breaking down social walls

Dave's impactful career decision

  • Started his comedy career when he was a teenager.
  • Had his own comedy show that made fun of stereotypes in a form of normalizing otherwise taboo topics.
  • Walked away from a $3mill+ deal due to some racial injustices that he brought to light years later.
  • He eases the tension of these injustices through comedy and brings down their guard to be receptive to his well thought description of the spoken joke/problem.
  • He does not care about political correctness when telling jokes, a refreshing voice in a climate extremely concerned with watching what you say.
  • He doesn't intend to be a movement leader, but has become one through the respect of those that respect him and his brashness.
